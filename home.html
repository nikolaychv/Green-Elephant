Our mission

Our mission is to protect elephants, to draw people's attention to their species and to protect and preserve them from extinction. Our goal is to preserve nature and the animals in it, as well as to develop a sense of empathy in people for the problem of the extinction of elephants.

Facts

  • Elephants are some of the smartest and most compassionate animals on the planet
  • Tens of thousands of elephants are being killed every year for their ivory tusks
  • Right now there are approximately 400,000 elephants left in the world (2019)
  • An estimated 100 African elephants are killed each day by poachers seeking ivory, meat and body parts
  • Despite a ban on the international trade in ivory, African elephants are still being poached in large numbers
  • Elephant numbers have dropped by 62% over the last decade, they could be mostly extinct by the end of the next decade
