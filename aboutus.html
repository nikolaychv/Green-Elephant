About us

GreenElephant is a site that seeks to draw people's attention to the problem of elephant extinction. Unfortunately, there are still about 400,000 elephants left in the world, and for comparison, between 2010 and 2012, about 100,000 elephants were killed. This led us to organize and create a website to encourage people to protect this species from extinction. Elephants are extremely intelligent and good animals that help the flora and fauna where they live. But unfortunately they are being persecuted and killed for their tusks. We, at GreenElephant, want to stop the extermination of elephants and to show people how they can help and empathize with their lives. We are convinced that people are capable of anything, when they're united by a common idea and strive to do good. That is why we are campaigning for the protection of animals and their habitats, for a better world for all of us!
