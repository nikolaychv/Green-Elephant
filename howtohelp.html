How to help elephants?

Getting Involved with Elephant-Protection Organizations

  1. Join a conservation organization
  2. Make a one-time or recurring donation
  3. Intern at an elephant sanctuary

Advocating and Raising Awareness for Elephant Causes

  1. Contact your representatives about existing legislation
  2. Sign petitions that protect elephants within your region
  3. Organize a letter-writing campaign
  4. Share information about elephants on social media

Taking Action to Protect Elephants

  1. Avoid products made with elephant ivory
  2. Choose products that protect an elephant's habitat
  3. Read about elephant conservation issues
  4. Avoid zoos, shelters, or shows where elephants aren't treated humanely
  5. Join protest movements
